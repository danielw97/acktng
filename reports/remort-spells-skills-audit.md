## Methodology

Each spell/skill was evaluated against:
- **Class lore** as defined in the help files (Chromatic Circle, Axiomatic Schools, Oathbound Vanguards, etc.)
- **Class identity** (stormbinder, precision arcanist, oathbound vanguard, etc.)
- **Mortal prerequisite class** progression (does the remort skill build on the mortal base?)
- **Mechanical role** (caster, healer, burst DPS, tank, hybrid)
- **Peer consistency** (do paired remorts from the same mortal share appropriate overlap vs. differentiation?)

Ratings: **Thematic** (fits perfectly), **Acceptable** (loose fit, tolerable), **Questionable** (weak thematic link), **Remove** (actively off-theme)

## Cross-Class Observations

### 1. Excessive Kit Overlap Between Paired Remorts

Several paired remort classes share too many abilities, weakening distinct identity:

| Pair | Shared Skills | Issue |
|------|--------------|-------|
| Sorcerer/Wizard | 16 of ~23 shared | Both have identical elemental/golem/shield suites. Wizard feels like "Sorcerer with haste." |
| Necromancer/Egomancer | ~15 shared | Egomancer is "Necromancer Lite + 3 psionic spells." Massive identity crisis. |
| Knight/Swordsman | 8 of ~14 shared | Better differentiated (sword skills vs. formation), but still share shadow/holy abilities that fit neither. |
| Monk/Brawler | 7 of ~11 shared | Reasonable overlap for unarmed base, but need more unique identity skills. |

### 2. Holy Magic on Secular Classes

`holy avenger` and `holystrike` appear on Knight and Swordsman — classes with explicitly secular, martial lore. These should be exclusive to Paladin/Priest.

### 3. Elemental Magic on Non-Elemental Classes

`Fire Blast`, `Shock Storm`, `heat armor`, and `rune:shock` appear on Necromancer and Egomancer — classes themed around entropy and psionics respectively. These elemental abilities belong on Sorcerer/Wizard.

### 4. "Frenzy" Thematic Mismatch

Frenzy (berserker AoE) appears on Priest (explicitly rejected combat) and Swordsman (explicitly disciplined technique). It fits Warden/Knight at best.

### 5. The "cage" Problem

`cage` (magical room-lock) appears on Assassin — a non-magical class. Either make it a physical trap for Assassin or remove it.

### 6. Egomancer Identity Crisis

Egomancer is the most problematic class. It shares the majority of Necromancer's kit (skeleton, black hand, soul net, condense soul, infuse, soul thief, all three curses) despite having completely different lore (mental imposition vs. entropy/death). This is the highest-priority rework needed.

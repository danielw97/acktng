# World Map: Lore-Precise Location Summation

Geographic placement for all areas in ACK!TNG, derived from canonical lore. All locations are described relative to each other and to the five major cities.

**Midgaard is the crossroads.** North leads through the Great Northern Forest to Kowloon. West leads down Roc Road to Kiess. South leads through the Eccentric Woodland to Rakuen. East leads through the Eastern Desert and Oasis-Pyramid Corridor to Mafdet. The far northwest holds the Cinderteeth Mountains at the edge of the accessible world.

---

## I. THE FIVE CITIES

**Midgaard** occupies the continental crossroads. North exit: Lantern Road into the Great Northern Forest. South gate: Eccentric Woodland toward Rakuen. West gate: Roc Road toward Kiess. East gate: desert caravan routes toward the Oasis-Pyramid Corridor. Beneath the city via the Violet Reliquary Arch lies the Gloamvault of Kel'Shadra; beneath the southern chapel quarter lie the Catacombs of Nightfall.

**Kowloon** sits at the far northern end of the continent, at the delta of the Jhen River. Reaching it from Midgaard requires traversing the entire Great Northern Forest (~400 square miles) via the Lantern Road. Iron Gate faces south toward the forest and Midgaard; Jade Gate faces north toward diplomatic sea routes; Harbor Gate faces east to the delta sea; Lantern Gate faces west toward farming territories.

**Kiess** lies at the far western end of the continent along Roc Road, built on the ruins of Evermeet. Roc Road is the only overland link to Midgaard. The Forest of Confusion forms Kiess's entire northern border.

**Rakuen** lies south of Midgaard, accessible only through the Eccentric Woodland. The city's North Dispatch Gate (room 4239) connects directly south from room 1830 at the woodland's southern terminus. The Redwater Braid flood channel bounds the city. No connections to other cities except north through Midgaard via the Woodland.

**Mafdet** lies on the eastern coast where the inland desert meets the sea, at the terminus of the Saltglass Reach. Reached by traveling east through the Eastern Desert, across the Oasis-Pyramid Corridor, and the full length of the Reach. The Shoreward Seal marks the legal boundary from inland to maritime jurisdiction. The only city with direct sea access.

---

## II. NORTHERN CORRIDOR: Midgaard to Kowloon

**Great Northern Forest** is the ~400-square-mile woodland belt lying directly between Midgaard (south) and Kowloon (north), traversed by the Lantern Road. Five sub-regions run sequentially north: Southern Verge (broadleaf, heaviest Midgaard influence, connects to the Granite Arcade) → Mosswater Bends (fenland basins, Mire-Speaker territory) → Ironpine Rise (elevated granite ridges, Everkeeper planting-rings) → Ancestor Deeps (oldest canopy, Cairn-Keeper megaliths, no sunlight) → Northern Crown (forest thins approaching Kowloon, Oath-Crossing neutral glade where both cities' charters were formalized).

**Thornwood (Harren Estate)** occupies the southern fringe exclusion zone of the Great Northern Forest, north of Midgaard's grave districts. It lies in the transition belt between Midgaard-linked mortuary routes and Shadowmere's southern approach. Its iron gate connects via a pre-civic grave tunnel to Shadowmere's southern approach. Excluded from normal Lantern Road contracts due to necrotic contamination.

**Shadowmere** lies north of Thornwood in the broader northern woodland belt — the frontier march beyond Thornwood's estate-forest. Reached from the south by passing through Thornwood and the shared grave tunnel hinge. Moving further north from Shadowmere reaches territory beyond organized city law.

---

## III. WESTERN CORRIDOR: Midgaard to Kiess

**Roc Road** runs due west from Midgaard's West Gate to Kiess through settled farmland, the Dustward Verge, River Ledger Crossing, and Banner Hills. The continent's primary east-west trade spine.

**Violet Eclipse Cathedral** stands along the western approaches from Midgaard toward Roc Road — close enough to the city's outer wards to be within magistrate supply range, far enough outside Watch circuits to host hazardous reliquary work. A threshold institution on Roc Road's eastern sections.

**Umbra Heartspire** sits mid-route along the western road between Midgaard and Kiess, a satellite archive-fortress positioned between the two cities. Its dual ancestry reflects its geography: Midgaard legal-archive culture to the east, Kiess/Evermeet funerary and aristocratic rites to the west.

**Forest of Confusion** lies directly north of Kiess, forming the city's entire northern frontier. The outer margin of the vast ancient woodland whose core is the Verdant Depths. Southern verge is manageable patrol zone; deeper north becomes a three-way collision between Kiess civic order, the Verdant ecological intelligence, and Withered Conclave residue — failed druidic quarantine and abandoned Spirebound Conclave crystal lattice experiments.

**Verdant Depths** occupy the deep interior of the Forest of Confusion, north and northwest of Kiess — the ancient living woodland whose distributed intelligence predates all human cities. The Forest of Confusion is its outer buffer zone; the Verdant Depths are the true core.

**Withered Depths** lie deep within the Forest of Confusion's interior, at the sites where the Spirebound Conclave implanted resonance crystals in ancient root junction nodes. The blight seeps outward from these original trial sites. The Blightmother occupies the Heartrot Grove at the deepest accessible point.

---

## IV. SOUTHERN FRONTIER: Midgaard to Rakuen

**Sepulcher Pasture** lies south of Midgaard just past the city's ninth milestone — the first landmark south of the Southern Wild Gate, occupying the old Ninth Acre burial grounds. Directly south leads into the Eccentric Woodland.

**Eccentric Woodland** fills the southern frontier immediately below Midgaard's southern gate, running south until it terminates at Rakuen's North Dispatch Gate. Unlike the managed Great Northern Forest, the Woodland is unchartered interior: paths shift, clearings change mood, isolated halls enforce local ritual codes. Midgaard claims gate authority but disclaims interior responsibility.

---

## V. EASTERN CORRIDOR: Midgaard to the Coast

**Eastern Desert** begins directly east of Midgaard's eastern gate. Five zones increase in danger and historical depth west-to-east: Zone A (charter stones, caravan road entry from Midgaard's east gate) → Zone B (cistern archives, administrative ruins) → Zone C (convoy fortifications, Reaver Confederate territory) → Zone D (Twelve Obelisks of Measure Script, Jackal Synod shrines) → Zone E (Last Sand Regent's preserved necropolis court, site of the failed Ninth Meridian eclipse rite). Connects northwest to Northern Oasis hunt-road approaches and south to the Great Oasis via Western Gate.

**Arroyo** is a canyon in the eastern desert corridor on the approaches toward the oasis cluster, a dry branch of the old Iseth watershed. Intersects caravan routes between the Eastern Desert's road network and the oasis triad.

**Scorched Wastes** occupy the zone between the oasis confluence and the pyramid approach routes — the old Meridian Intake March. Located east of the oasis triad and west/southwest of the pyramid complex, on the connecting routes between the two.

**Scorching Sands** occupy the east-southeastern quadrant of the Eastern Desert, the transitional wedge between the interior caravan spine and the coastward Saltglass corridors. West/northwest boundary meets the Eastern Desert's late obelisk grid; southwest meets Southern Oasis convoy lanes; east meets the Saltglass Reach's Heavy Road cairn line; south meets faulted heat pans. Approximately 2-3 caravan days east-southeast of the Great Oasis and 1 day west of the first Saltglass mirror-flat bands.

**Great Oasis** sits southeast of the Eastern Desert's main road spine, at the primary vent of the Deepwell Confluence aquifer — the calibration hub of the Oasis-Pyramid Corridor. Northern Oasis lies north; Southern Oasis lies south. All three share the same aquifer.

**Northern Oasis** lies north of the Great Oasis, at the tertiary (smallest, most brackish) fracture of the Deepwell Confluence. The corridor's northern intake and verification node.

**Southern Oasis** lies south of the Great Oasis, at the secondary fracture of the Deepwell Confluence. The corridor's convoy hardening and expedition marshalling node — last point of civil order before the Southern Pyramid frontier. Southern Pyramid approach routes originate here.

**Lost City (Khepra-Lesh)** lies within the Oasis-Pyramid Corridor between the oasis cluster and the pyramid complex, sustained by a subsidiary branch of the Deepwell Confluence. The hinge-city where oasis water-law, pyramid funerary jurisdiction, and Eastern Desert caravan law overlapped.

**Khar'Daan (Sunken Necropolis)** occupies a mineral fault in the desert corridor between the oasis cluster and the pyramid routes, where warm brackish water rises beneath sandstone shelves. Accessible from the desert routes connecting oases to pyramids.

**Sultan's Palace** stands within the Oasis-Pyramid Corridor on the seam between the oasis triad and the pyramid sphere, a secondary adjudication capital drawing authority from both the Deepwell Confluence water traditions and southern pyramid mortuary doctrine.

**Akh'enet (City of the Eclipsed Ledger)** sits between the Southern Oasis and the Southern Pyramid approach roads, in the southern corridor of the Oasis-Pyramid arc. The legal-mortuary relay where caravans, bodies, debt, relics, and verdicts transitioned between oasis water law and pyramid funerary authority.

**Northern Pyramid** stands in the northeastern arm of the pyramid complex, northeast of the oasis cluster. Built by the Sand Sovereign (Solar Court lineage) with a seven-floor ascending structure. Its astrolabe chambers descend from the obelisk-calibration tradition of Zone D in the Eastern Desert.

**Southern Pyramid** stands in the southeastern arm of the pyramid complex, southeast of the oasis cluster, above a geological fault connected to the Iseth Reach's deep aquifer. Built by the Moon Sovereign (Sand Sovereign's twin rival) with a seven-tier descending structure consecrated to aspects of death, inverting the Northern Pyramid's ascending solar doctrine.

**Great Pyramid** stands at the far eastern end of the desert complex, accessible from Southern Oasis approach routes and the Eastern Desert's deep interior. The most ancient pyramid structure and the site of the Black Sun Shard's containment. Its administrative network created the Water Court system inherited by all three oases.

**Saltglass Reach** begins at the eastern margins of the oasis-pyramid caravan basin and runs east to the coast. Four bands west-to-east: Mirror Flats (hard salt crust, Iseth evaporation remnant) → Glasswind Belts (wind-cut silica shards from the Ninth Meridian thermal pulse; glass color indicates ground stability) → Tariff Cairn Lines (three parallel ruined checkpoint chains: Swift Route north, Heavy Road center, Sealed Route south) → Tidemouth Dunes (brackish coastal approach ending at the Shoreward Seal and Mafdet's maritime jurisdiction).

## VI. KEL'SHADRA INSTITUTIONAL COMPLEX

Three connected sites organized hierarchically from public-facing to deep-coercive, all accessible via underground networks centered beneath Midgaard.

**Gloamvault of Kel'Shadra** lies beneath Midgaard, accessed via the Violet Reliquary Arch in the civic core. The public-doctrinal layer: confession intake, oath classification, liturgical authority.

**Catacombs of Nightfall** lie beneath Midgaard's southern chapel quarter, accessed through the Ossuary Transport Court. Geographically clustered with the Gloamvault and Dungeon Intake Yard within the same subterranean administrative zone.

**Crypts of Kel'Shadra** occupy the deep enforcement stratum below the Gloamvault — the terminal filtration layer between the Gloamvault's public doctrine and the Void Citadel's recurrence machinery. Kel'Shadra's disappearance logistics center.

**Void Citadel of Kel'Shadra** is the recurrence-enforcement apex — a physically distinct site from the Gloamvault and Crypts, in a more remote or fortified location accessed via the same underground network. Built around a vertical paradox (spire above, catacomb below); the high-command complex where testimony was iterated until it produced politically acceptable outcomes.

---

## VII. BOUNDARY AND SUPPLEMENTARY AREAS

**Sunken Sanctum** occupies a deep fold in the western spur of the Cinderteeth Mountains — the volcanic range at the far northwest edge of the accessible continental map. The Spirebound Conclave chose this location for geothermal forges, volcanic glass supply, and remoteness from lowland politics. The Cinderteeth's eruptions caused the Ash Winters devastating the Great Northern Forest and the Ashfall Monsoon that struck Kowloon's delta.

**Wizard's Keep** is a remote, high-elevation adept training site at the accessible world's edge, in a forest-and-mountain setting isolated from the main continental routes.

## IX. CARDINAL PRECISION NOTES

- **Eccentric Woodland is due south of Midgaard.** Room 1830 (woodland south end) connects south to room 4239 (Rakuen's North Dispatch Gate). Rakuen is the southernmost city.
- **Great Northern Forest is due north of Midgaard and due south of Kowloon.** The Lantern Road is the only overland connection. No bypass exists.
- **Thornwood is north of Midgaard but south of the main forest travel band** — southern fringe exclusion zone. Shadowmere is north of Thornwood. Neither is on the Lantern Road's normal route.
- **Three oases form a cluster**: Great Oasis at center (primary vent), Northern Oasis north (tertiary vent), Southern Oasis south (secondary vent). All three share the Deepwell Confluence aquifer.
- **Three pyramids mirror the oasis cluster's axis**: Northern Pyramid (NE desert), Southern Pyramid (SE desert), Great Pyramid (far E, before the Saltglass Reach).
- **Kiess is due west; Mafdet is due east** of Midgaard. North-south axis: Kowloon (N) → Midgaard → Rakuen (S).
- **Cinderteeth Mountains are far northwest**, beyond Kiess. Sunken Sanctum is in their western spur.
- **Saltglass Reach runs east** from the oasis-pyramid basin to the coast. Mafdet is due east of the pyramids.
- **Forest of Confusion is north of Kiess**, not between Kiess and Midgaard. Verdant Depths and Withered Depths are both deep inside the Forest of Confusion.

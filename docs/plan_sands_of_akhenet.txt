2. THEME AND LORE
================================================================================

  The Sands of Akh'enet are a cursed stretch of deep desert surrounding the
  buried city-temple of Akh'enet, once a thriving civilization devoted to the
  sun god Solahr. When the high priest Amenthari attempted a ritual to achieve
  godhood, the resulting magical cataclysm scorched the land, buried the city,
  and cursed its inhabitants to undeath. Now the ruins slowly emerge from
  shifting dunes, drawing treasure seekers, raiders, and worse.

  The area is divided into five distinct zones connected by a central hub:

    (A) The Blasted Dunes       - Open desert approaches (entry zone)
    (B) The Sunken Bazaar       - Half-buried merchant district
    (C) The Temple of Solahr    - Above-ground temple complex
    (D) The Buried Catacombs    - Underground tomb network
    (E) The Sanctum of Amenthari - Deep inner sanctum (boss zone)

12. DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

  - Mob levels scale from 70 (entry dunes) to 90 (final boss), providing
    a smooth difficulty curve across the five zones.
  - Boss mobs all have sentinel+boss flags and are placed in no_mob rooms
    per the area spec requirements.
  - Equipment AC and hit/dam modifiers scale with level, following the
    patterns established in the Scorching Sands (29900) reference area.
  - The area has two shopkeepers for player convenience (supplies + gear).
  - Color theming uses @@y (yellow) for desert outdoor rooms, @@b (brown)
    for underground/ruin rooms, and @@R (red) for boss encounters.
  - 75 unused vnums (2075-2149) are reserved for future expansion such as
    additional quest mobs, side chambers, or event content.
  - The locked temple vault door adds a minor key-finding objective that
    gates access to the deeper catacomb content.
  - Elemental strengths/weaknesses on bosses encourage varied group
    compositions and spell choices.

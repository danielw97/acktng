2. THEME AND LORE
================================================================================

  The Sands of Akh'enet are a cursed stretch of deep desert surrounding the
  buried city-temple of Akh'enet, once a thriving civilization devoted to the
  sun god Solahr. When the high priest Amenthari attempted a ritual to achieve
  godhood, the resulting magical cataclysm scorched the land, buried the city,
  and cursed its inhabitants to undeath. Now the ruins slowly emerge from
  shifting dunes, drawing treasure seekers, raiders, and worse.

  The area is divided into eight distinct zones connected by a central hub:

    (A) The Blasted Dunes       - Open desert approaches (entry zone)
    (B) The Sunken Bazaar       - Half-buried merchant district
    (C) The Temple of Solahr    - Above-ground temple complex
    (D) The Buried Catacombs    - Underground tomb network
    (E) The Sanctum of Amenthari - Deep inner sanctum (boss zone)
    (F) The Serpent Caverns     - Underground caves teeming with reptiles
    (G) The Raider Warcamp      - Fortified desert bandit encampment
    (H) The Oasis of Shadows    - A corrupted oasis of dark water magic

11. DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

  - Mob levels scale from 70 (entry dunes) to 95 (final boss Amenthari),
    providing a smooth difficulty curve across all eight zones.
  - 7 boss mobs total, ranging from level 90 to 95:
      The Serpent Queen (90), Vizier Nephkara (91), Sandstorm Colossus (91),
      Warchief Ghazaan (92), The Devourer (93), Sha'ithara (93),
      High Priest Amenthari (95).
  - All boss mobs have sentinel+boss flags and are placed in no_mob rooms
    per the area spec requirements.
  - Item levels are 75-85 for trash drops and 90 for all boss drops.
  - Boss drops use ITEM_RARE flag for enhanced rarity.
  - No shopkeepers in the area; players must bring their own supplies or
    find food/drink utility objects scattered in rooms.
  - Color theming uses @@y (yellow) for desert outdoor rooms, @@b (brown)
    for underground/ruin rooms, and @@R (red) for boss encounters.
  - Three locked doors gate access to treasure rooms, each requiring a key
    found elsewhere in the same zone.
  - 25 unused vnums (2125-2149) are reserved for future expansion.
  - Elemental strengths/weaknesses on bosses encourage varied group
    compositions and spell choices:
      Serpent Queen: strong poison, weak fire
      Vizier Nephkara: strong fire, weak water
      Sandstorm Colossus: strong earth, weak air
      Warchief Ghazaan: strong physical, weak mental
      The Devourer: strong shadow, weak holy
      Sha'ithara: strong shadow+water, weak holy+fire
      Amenthari: strong fire+holy, weak shadow

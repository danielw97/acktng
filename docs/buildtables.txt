Itemization Points: 10 + item level
Rare item: If true rare, +50% extra points
Piece item: +5% extra points per common piece, +50% extra points per rare piece, not multiplicative

Default stats:

Weapon: 5 hr/dr + item_level/30. Adept is base of 45 hr/dr.
Armor: -10 AC - item_level. Adept is base of -130.
Jewelry: 10 hp/mana + 10 hp/mana/move per 10 levels. Adept is base of 130 hp/mana and 120 move.

Item Stat Costs:
1 point for hr, dr
1 point for -5 AC
1 point for 5 hp, mana, move
10 points for 1 str, mana, cha, wis, con, max of 1 total unless item is rare, at which point max is 5.
